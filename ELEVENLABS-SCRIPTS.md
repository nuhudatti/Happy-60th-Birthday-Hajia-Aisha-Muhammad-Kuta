# ElevenLabs voice scripts — Hajia Aisha Muhammad Kuta 60th Birthday

Use these in **ElevenLabs** (or any TTS) to generate the two audio files. Export as **MP3** and save into the `assets/` folder.

---

## 1. INTRO AUDIO (first page, bottom-right, auto-plays)

**Filename to save as:** `assets/intro-audio.mp3`  
**Use for:** The cinematic first screen. Soft, calm, hopeful.  
**Voice suggestion:** Choose a warm, calm narrator (e.g. “Rachel” or “Adam”) — not too loud, so it feels like a film opening.

**Script to paste into ElevenLabs:**

```
Every great life writes its story in the stars. Today we celebrate a mother whose love shaped our world. Happy 60th birthday, Mum. Hajia Aisha Muhammad Kuta. Mother of Wisdom. Matriarch of Legacy. Heart of the Ndatti Dynasty. [Pause 2 seconds.] Tap Enter to step into her universe.
```

**Optional shorter version** (if you want a quicker loop):

```
Every great life writes its story in the stars. Today we celebrate a mother whose love shaped our world. Happy 60th birthday, Mum.
```

---

## 2. MESSAGE FROM MY HEART (personal voice message)

**Filename to save as:** `assets/voice-message.mp3`  
**Use for:** The “Listen to a message from my heart” button.  
**Voice suggestion:** Use **your own cloned voice** in ElevenLabs so it really sounds like you speaking to her. Speak slowly, with pauses and emotion.

**Script to paste into ElevenLabs (customize the name if you want):**

```
Mum, it’s me. I wanted the whole world to hear this, because what you’ve given us deserves to be said out loud.

You didn’t just raise children. You raised a family that stands together, that remembers where it came from, and that carries your name with pride. You taught us that strength isn’t about being the loudest in the room — it’s about being the calm in the storm. That love isn’t just words; it’s every meal you made, every prayer you said, and every sacrifice we only understood when we grew up.

You are the heart of our home. The soul of the Ndatti Dynasty. And today, on your 60th birthday, I want you to know: we see you. We honour you. And we love you more than any words on a page or any gift could ever show.

Happy 60th birthday, Mum. May the years ahead be full of the same peace and joy you’ve given us. Thank you for everything. We love you.
```

**Shorter alternative** (if you want something under 1 minute):

```
Mum, I wanted the world to hear this. You didn’t just raise us — you gave us a legacy. You taught us that real strength is quiet, and that love shows up in every meal, every prayer, and every sacrifice. You are the heart of our family and the soul of the Ndatti Dynasty. On your 60th birthday, we see you, we honour you, and we love you. Happy birthday, Mum. Thank you for everything.
```

---

## After generating

1. **Intro (auto-play):** Save as `assets/intro-audio.mp3`. The site will try to play it on the first page; on some phones one tap may be needed to allow sound.
2. **Message from my heart:** Save as `assets/voice-message.mp3`. It will play when she taps “Listen to a message from my heart”.
